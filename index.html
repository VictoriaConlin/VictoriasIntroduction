A few of the biggest things I learned throughout this experience were; I love working on a team, I am great at learning and growing when I get thrown into a challenge, communication is key, and understanding the "why" is always important.

Why Web Development?

When we got the opportunity to sell our business I started thinking about what I wanted to do for my future career. In all of my experiences I found that I enjoy working on a team and collaborating with others. I was good at figuring out solutions to problems and making "things" do what I want them to do (ie. robots, 3D models, getting a phone to work). I like learning new things and being thrown into a situation and growing from it. So when someone suggested that I enroll in a codding bootcamp I decided to take on the challenge and try something new. I loved the experience. I found that my favorite part of web development was that moment when you conquer a problem and get your project to do what you want. I am very excited to have so many more of those moments in my Web Development career.
